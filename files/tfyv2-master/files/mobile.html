Welcome to Tactify


Tactify is a new tool to help you integrate your online content with your print marketing. Tactify uses NFC and QR to let you communicate with all modern smartphones and tablets.

Tactify takes the hassle out of your mobile content using templates that are designed to operate and display intuitively on all mobile operating systems. Using Tactify's management system you can monitor and track all user interactions and ordering NFC and QR enabled marketing collateral is quick and easy.
